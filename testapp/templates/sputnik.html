Sputnik

How efficacious is the vaccine?

Vaccine efficacy based on the numbers of confirmed COVID-19 cases from 21 days after the first dose of vaccine, is reported as 91·6% (95% CI 85·6–95·2)

What is the recommended dosage?

Vaccine experts recommended a maximum dose interval of 42 days. It was annnounced on September 4, 2021, that the Sputnik V has a booster shot for those 18 and above who received their 2nd dose of the Sputnik V vaccine at least 6 months ago.

The Sputnik V is currently authorized for use in 68 countries. It was developed by Gamaleya National Center of Epidemiology and Microbiology in Moscow, Russia. It is a viral vector vaccine that produce immunity against COVID-19. A viral vector is a harmless virus that can deliver a gene to our cells that they turn into a protein.
