CoronaVac

How efficacious is the vaccine?

Trial in Brazil showed that two doses with interval of 14 days has efficacy of 51% against symptomatic SARS-CoV-2 infection, 100% against severe COVID-19, and 100% against hospitalization.

What is the recommended dosage?

Sinovac-CoronaVac vaccine has 2 doses (0.5 ml) given intramuscularly. WHO recommends an interval of 2–4 weeks between the first and second dose. It is recommended that all vaccinated individuals receive two doses.

Individuals with a history of anaphylaxis to any component of the vaccine should not take it. Persons with acute PCR-confirmed COVID-19 should not be vaccinated. Anyone with a body temperature over 38.5°C should postpone vaccination until they no longer have a fever.
