Moderna

How efficacious is the vaccine?

The Moderna vaccine has been shown to have an efficacy of approximately 94.1 per cent in protecting against COVID-19, starting 14 days after the first dose.

What is the recommended dosage?

Moderna mRNA-1273 vaccine at a schedule of two doses 28 days apart. If necessary, the interval may be extended to 42 days.

On 30 April, WHO listed the Moderna vaccine for emergency use. It is safe with comorbidities such as; chronic lung disease, significant cardiac disease, severe obesity, diabetes, liver disease and human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) infection. Individuals with a history of severe allergic reaction to any component of the vaccine should not take this or any other mRNA vaccine.
