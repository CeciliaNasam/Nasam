AstraZeneca

How efficacious is the vaccine?

The AZD1222 vaccine against COVID-19 has an efficacy of 63.09% against symptomatic SARS-CoV-2 infection.

What is the recommended dosage?

Two dosage are recommended. Longer dose intervals within the 8 to 12 weeks range are associated with greater vaccine efficacy.

As of 19 April 2021, the AstraZeneca vaccine is safe and effective at protecting people from the extremely serious risks of COVID-19, including death, hospitalization and severe disease. The World Health Organization's Global Advisory Committee on Vaccine Safety reports of very rare side effects.
